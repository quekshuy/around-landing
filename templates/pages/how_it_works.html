What do I do with around! ?

Download FREE

around! is an application on the iPhone that allows users to collect loyalty stamps on loyalty cards from merchants. You just have to scan a QR code on the merchant's premises and earn rewards for patronising, stamp by stamp. Never have to stuff loyalty cards in your wallet ever again.

Sssh. Some merchants even offer discounts and promotions that can be enjoyed only if you use around! to redeem them.
So download the app now - it's FREE.
